Typography Projects

These projects were made for my typography class where we explored how to use the shape of text to experiment with different emotional appeals.

The Evolution of the Design

I will describe this final draft with how it makes me feel. From the fun and colorful life of the fantasy world we are met at a point in the depth of the narrative. In each well told story we can read between the lines to see what is hidden in the shadows. But it is just a story, nothing is real but the lie that fiction tells. However, the vibrant colors on stage shed light into the black and white of good and evil. If we look closley enough we will find truth in the white of the lie. Coming to the realization that fantasy at its best is in unison with reality makes the story worth watching which is any artists hope for their piece.

This project is one of my favorties. The point of it was to represent scale in the shape. If you look at the letters in the middle you can see the "X w Y m v" extending out on both sides. What is cool about it is you can see the physical letters in white (X Y v) get smaller as you go outward. However, If you look at the tops of those letters it looks like a bunch of v's side by side which don't change size. That is just one example of whats going on within this piece.

This project came to mind when us students were given the task of making letters seem playful. which I think this design conveys that really well. My thinking was to use only letters to make a playground and use all capital letters only. However, with the letters representing children on a playground are all lowercase. I thought that symbolisim of adults being the foundation and "bigger letters" while the children are the playful and colorful "little letters" was an interesting concept and I am happy with how it turned out.
